Dill Pickled Deviled Eggs

These dill pickle deviled eggs will surprise your taste buds with the salty, briny tang from dill pickle juice and a little crunch from the pickles.

Ingredients

  • 6 large hard boiled eggs, peeled
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise, or more if needed
  • 2 teaspoons dill pickle juice
  • 2 teaspoons yellow mustard
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons finely minced dill pickle, well drained
  • fresh dill sprigs, for garnish

Steps

  1. Slice eggs in half lengthwise, remove yolks, and set egg whites aside.
  2. Add crumbled yolks, mayonnaise, pickle juice, mustard, salt, and pepper to a small bowl, and mash with a fork until smooth; stir in pickles.
  3. Place egg whites cut side up on a serving platter. Evenly spoon or pipe mixture into each egg white, and garnish with fresh dill. Cover, and refriegerate until ready to serve, at least 1 hour.
